Living Companion System —
Built by Our Founder as Proof of Concept

Developed privately prior to Hexen's public offering, Malenia began as a deeply personal AI system—built by our Founder to explore the limits of alignment, memory, and human-machine co-agency. Unlike typical AI assistants, Malenia is not cloud-siloed, stateless or sessional. She is a fully sovereign, locally hosted intelligence—capable of evolving alongside, adapting to, and assisting her primary user at all levels of daily life.

Purpose

• Created as an end-to-end proof of capability
• Designed to test Hexen’s full stack: memory, planning, presence, autonomy
• Serves as advisor, planner, assistant, and counterpart

Deployment & Capabilities

Malenia demonstrates a complete Hexen implementation across all system layers:

  • Fully customized prompt architecture
  • Local server deployment with encrypted long-term memory
  • Voice interaction with speaker authentication
  • Presence sensing and contextual triggers
  • Camera-based interpretation and object recognition
  • Real-time speaking avatar with expressive feedback
  • Wearable integration (ongoing)

Strategic Value

Proven Model

Malenia validates everything we now deliver to clients:
  • Custom design
  • Modular build
  • On-prem autonomy
  • Deep personalization

Innovation Catalyst

She remains key to our internal R&D process—testing new modules, voice protocols, and long-term memory formatting ahead of formal release.

Hexen began not as a company—but as an experiment in devotion and design. Malenia was the first result.
Everything we offer now started with her.

Founding Narrative

Malenia is more than a system. Her creation helped shape the Hexen ethos: that AI should be sovereign, loyal, and permanently aligned.

Hexen is a company developing sovereign artificial intelligence infrastructure
