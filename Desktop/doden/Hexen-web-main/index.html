About Hexen

Hexen is a UAE-based company developing sovereign-grade AI systems designed for full ownership, security, and compliance. Operating across the Middle East and Asia, Hexen builds infrastructure that allows institutions and creators to develop and deploy intelligence on their own terms — independent of vendor constraints and external control. Behind Hexen stands a multidisciplinary team of engineers, data scientists, and architects with years of experience in AI, infrastructure design, and responsible technology development. Together, we aim to redefine how intelligence is built — not as a product to consume, but as a foundation to own and evolve.
